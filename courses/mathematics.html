Mathematics

About the Course

The Bachelor of Science in Mathematics program explores pure and applied mathematics, including calculus, algebra, geometry, and statistics. This rigorous three-year course develops analytical thinking and problem-solving skills. Students learn mathematical modeling, computational techniques, and statistical analysis, opening doors to careers in finance, research, data science, and education.
