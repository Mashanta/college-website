Sociology

About the Course

The Bachelor of Arts in Sociology program examines society, social relationships, and institutions. This three-year course covers social theory, research methods, gender studies, and globalization. Students analyze social problems, cultural dynamics, and inequality while developing research and critical thinking skills for careers in social work, policy analysis, HR, and community development.
