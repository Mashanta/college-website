Economics

About the Course

The Bachelor of Arts in Economics program analyzes production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services. This three-year course combines microeconomics, macroeconomics, econometrics, and development economics. Students develop quantitative skills, policy analysis capabilities, and critical thinking for careers in banking, finance, government, research, and international organizations.
