Computer Science

About the Course

Our Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program provides a comprehensive foundation in computing principles, programming, and technology. The three-year degree course covers data structures, algorithms, database management, web development, and software engineering. Students gain hands-on experience with modern programming languages and development tools, preparing them for careers in software development, data analysis, and IT consulting.
