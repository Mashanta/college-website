Chemistry

About the Course

The B.Sc. Chemistry program offers comprehensive study of organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry. This three-year degree combines theoretical knowledge with extensive hands-on laboratory experience. Students explore chemical synthesis, analytical techniques, and modern instrumentation, preparing for careers in pharmaceuticals, research, quality control, and chemical engineering.
