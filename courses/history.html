History

About the Course

The Bachelor of Arts in History program explores human civilization from ancient times to the contemporary era. This three-year course examines political, social, economic, and cultural transformations across the world. Students develop critical analysis, research, and writing skills while studying historiography, archaeology, and archival methods, preparing for careers in education, museums, journalism, and civil services.
