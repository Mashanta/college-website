Finance

About the Course

The Bachelor of Commerce in Finance program provides expertise in financial management, investment analysis, and banking operations. This three-year course covers corporate finance, financial markets, risk management, and portfolio analysis. Students develop skills in financial modeling, stock analysis, and banking regulations, preparing for careers in investment banking, financial planning, corporate finance, and wealth management.
