English Literature

About the Course

The Bachelor of Arts in English Literature program offers an immersive journey through literary traditions from Shakespeare to contemporary writers. This three-year course develops critical thinking, analytical writing, and communication skills. Students explore poetry, drama, novels, and literary theory while understanding cultural contexts, preparing for careers in publishing, journalism, education, and content creation.

  • Prof. Vikram Seth - M.A., Postcolonial Literature Specialist
  • Dr. Rebecca Moore - Ph.D., American Literature & Poetry
  • Prof. Arundhati Roy - M.A., Indian Writing in English
  • Mr. Dhruv Patel - M.A., Drama & Theatre Studies
