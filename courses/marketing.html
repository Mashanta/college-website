Marketing

About the Course

The Bachelor of Commerce in Marketing program focuses on consumer behavior, brand management, and digital marketing strategies. This three-year course covers market research, advertising, sales management, and social media marketing. Students gain practical skills through live projects, internships, and case studies, preparing for careers in brand management, digital marketing, market research, and advertising agencies.
