Psychology

About the Course

The Bachelor of Arts in Psychology program investigates human behavior and mental processes. This three-year course covers cognitive psychology, developmental psychology, abnormal psychology, and counseling techniques. Students learn research methodologies, psychological assessment, and therapeutic approaches, preparing for careers in mental health, HR, social work, and clinical research.
