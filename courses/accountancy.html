Accountancy

About the Course

The Bachelor of Commerce in Accountancy program provides comprehensive training in financial accounting, cost accounting, and auditing. This three-year course covers accounting principles, tax laws, financial management, and corporate reporting. Students develop practical skills in Tally, GST compliance, and financial analysis, preparing for careers as chartered accountants, tax consultants, financial analysts, and auditors.
