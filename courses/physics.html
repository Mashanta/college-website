Physics

About the Course

Our B.Sc. Physics program delves into the fundamental laws of nature, from classical mechanics to quantum physics and relativity. The three-year course covers electromagnetism, thermodynamics, optics, and nuclear physics. With extensive laboratory work and modern equipment, students develop experimental skills and theoretical understanding for careers in research, engineering, and technology.
