Business Studies

About the Course

The Bachelor of Commerce in Business Studies program provides a holistic understanding of business organizations and management principles. This three-year course covers business law, organizational behavior, entrepreneurship, and strategic management. Students develop leadership, communication, and decision-making skills through case studies and projects, preparing for careers in management consulting, HR, and business development.
